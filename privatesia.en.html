What data the site collects

Orders on WhatsApp

Orders and questions happen over WhatsApp. When you message us there, the conversation is governed by WhatsApp's (Meta's) own terms and privacy policy. We use your number and messages only to reply and prepare your order; we do not share them with anyone else.

Your rights

Under Albanian personal data protection law, you may request information about, correction of, or deletion of the data we hold from conversations with you. Just contact us at the number above.
