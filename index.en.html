Intimo per tutti

Quality intimates for women and men

IntimoBruna is a family-run intimates shop in the centre of Durrës, opened in April 2022 by Brunila Boraj. We keep underwear for women and men in a wide range of sizes, from everyday basics to something a little special, and we add new arrivals all the time.

Come in and have a look, or send us a message on WhatsApp and we will help you find the right fit.

  • Women and men

    Everyday underwear and lingerie for the whole family, in one shop.

  • A wide range of sizes

    From smaller cups to fuller figures, with help finding your fit in store.

  • New arrivals often

    Fresh styles come in through the week, so there is always something new.
