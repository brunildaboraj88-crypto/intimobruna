Lingerie as a gift: a simple guide to a special present

July 5, 2026 · 5 min read

Quick answer

Find the size without spoiling the surprise by checking the label on a bra they wear often, or choose nightwear that forgives size mistakes. Pick for their taste and leave the wrapping to us, and we'll exchange it if the size is off.

Lingerie is an intimate, thoughtful gift, but many people are a little afraid to buy it. With a bit of care it becomes a present that makes an impression. Here's how to pull it off without the stress.

Find the size without spoiling the surprise

Size is everyone's first worry. A few simple tricks:

  • Quietly check the label on a bra they wear often (e.g. 75B, 80C).
  • For briefs and nightwear, S/M/L sizes are more forgiving.
  • If you're unsure, choose nightwear or a set with bottoms: they forgive size mistakes.

Choose for the person, not for you

Think about their taste, not yours:

  • For someone classic: neutral colours and delicate lace.
  • For someone bold: red or burgundy.
  • For comfort: nightwear in silk or soft cotton.
The best gift isn't the most expensive one, it's the one that shows you put thought into it.

Don't forget the wrapping

Careful wrapping makes the gift even more special. Add a small note with a few words from the heart, those are often what's remembered most.

When you're not sure, ask

You don't have to decide alone. Tell us about the person and the occasion and we'll suggest ideas that fit. And if the size isn't right, we're here to exchange it, no trouble.

At IntimoBruna we'll help you choose the right gift and wrap it with care. Come by the shop in Durrës or message us on WhatsApp for ideas.
