How to choose swimwear for summer in Durrës

July 12, 2026 · 5 min read

Quick answer

Choose by body shape and comfort: push-up to flatter the neckline, side ties for fuller hips, underwire for support. Always try it on before buying. See our swimwear or come by the shop in Durrës.

Summer in Durrës means long days at the beach, evenings by the sea and the need for swimwear that fits well and makes you feel confident. But with so many shapes and styles, how do you pick the right one? Here are a few simple tips we use every day with our customers.

Start with your body shape

There's no "wrong" style, just the one that plays to your strengths. A few ideas:

  • To flatter the neckline: push-up styles or triangle tops with light padding.
  • For fuller hips: bottoms with side ties that adjust easily and sit comfortably.
  • For a fuller bust: underwire bikinis with slightly wider straps for all-day support.
  • For a straight silhouette: polka dots, ruffles or side details that create shape and movement.

Choose colour and style

Dark colours like black and burgundy are slimming and always elegant. Polka dots are a timeless classic, while warm tones like red or brown look great on sun-kissed skin. Pick a colour you feel like yourself in.

The best swimwear is the kind you put on without a second thought, because it makes you feel good.

Don't overlook comfort

A pretty bikini that bothers you isn't worth it. When you try it on, pay attention to the details:

  • Straps shouldn't dig in and should adjust well.
  • Ties should be strong and secure in the water.
  • A fabric that dries quickly and doesn't fade from sun and salt.

Always try it on before buying and move around a little: sit down, raise your arms, see how it sits.

One set is not enough

Ideally you'll have at least two: one for swimming and moving, and a "nicer" one for the beach or pool. That way you always have a dry one ready, and each lasts longer.
