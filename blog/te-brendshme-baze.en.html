The underwear basics that should never be missing from your drawer

July 9, 2026 · 6 min read

Quick answer

The basics worth having in any wardrobe: a nude bra and a black one, a wireless one for comfort, a few cotton briefs and, for men, neutral boxers. Find it all in our collections or at the shop in Durrës.

A good wardrobe starts with your underwear. When you have the right basics, every outfit sits better and your day feels more comfortable. Here's our list of underwear basics, for women and men, that's worth always having on hand.

For her: the basic bras

You don't need dozens, just a few well-chosen pieces that cover every occasion:

  • A nude bra: "invisible" under any light top.
  • A classic black bra: goes with everything.
  • A wireless bra: for all-day comfort on long days.
  • A push-up: for open necklines or special occasions.
  • A sports bra: for training and movement.

For her: the briefs

  • Cotton briefs for everyday, comfortable and breathable.
  • A few seamless pairs for tight dresses and trousers.
  • A lace set, to feel special when you want to.

For him: boxers and vests

  • Cotton boxers, a few pairs, in neutral colours (black, blue, grey).
  • Plain white vests, to wear under a shirt.
  • Better quality is worth it: it lasts longer and sits better.
Good underwear doesn't show, it simply makes everything else look better.

How often should you replace them?

A frequently worn bra loses its shape after about a year; when the elastic gives out or the wire bothers you, it's time to replace it. Briefs get replaced when they fade or lose their shape. One simple tip: buy a few pieces at a time and rotate them, so they last longer.
